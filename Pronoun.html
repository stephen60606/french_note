Articles and Pronouns

Indefinite Articles

  • Singular: Un, Une => Plural: Des

Subject Pronouns

  • I: Je, J’ (before vowel) => We: Nous
  • You (singular): Tu => You (plural/formal): Vous
  • He: Il => They (masculine): Ils
  • She: Elle => They (feminine): Elles
  • We (informal): On

Relative Pronouns

  • Qui = who/which/that (subject)

    Becomes qu’ before a vowel.

  • Que = who/whom/which/that (object)

    Becomes qu’ before a vowel, often followed by a noun.

  • Dont = of whom/whose/which (object/person with verb)

    e.g., La femme dont je parle (The woman I’m talking about).

  • Où = where/when (place or time)

    e.g., La ville où j’habite (The city where I live).
