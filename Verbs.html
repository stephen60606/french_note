French Basic Verbs - Two Days Notes

Common Verbs with Pronouns

Conjugations primarily in the present tense, with examples for each pronoun.

Adorer (to love) - Regular -er Verb

PronounConjugationExample
Jej’adoreJ’adore le chocolat. (I love chocolate.)
Tutu adoresTu adores danser. (You love to dance.)
Il/Elle/Onil/elle/on adoreElle adore les fleurs. (She loves flowers.)
Nousnous adoronsNous adorons voyager. (We love to travel.)
Vousvous adorezVous adorez la musique. (You love music.)
Ils/Ellesils/elles adorentIls adorent jouer. (They love to play.)
