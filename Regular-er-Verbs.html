Regular -ER Verbs

Conjugation Pattern for -ER Verbs

Regular verbs ending in -ER follow this pattern in the present tense. Remove the -ER from the infinitive to get the stem, then add the appropriate ending based on the subject pronoun.

SubjectEnding
Je (I)-e
Tu (You)-es
Il/Elle/On (He/She/We)-e
Nous (We)-ons
Vous (You)-ez
Ils/Elles (They)-ent

Example: Parler (to speak) → Je parle, Tu parles, Il parle, Nous parlons, Vous parlez, Ils parlent
